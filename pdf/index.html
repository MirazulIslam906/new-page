What is a P.D.? (Pupillary Distance)

The Pupillary Distance (P.D.) is the distance between the centers of your pupils. This measurement is used to manufacture Prescription Eyeglasses, to keep your vision as accurate as possible. The measurement is usually given in Millimeters.
